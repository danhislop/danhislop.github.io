About The Songs

There's a song here for every kind of day - from the opening strands of New Day Come and the energized Tallest Signpost to the cleverly simple closing tune If I Were You. A sad but hopeful Day to Day remembers a friend going through addiction recovery but stumbling along the way. First Love confesses "I forgot" while Laughing Soon anticipates the arrival of a special gift. The title track celebrates having nothing, through the eyes of a family packing up to do relief work in Africa.

A good focused listen is what you want. Here are a few samples to get you started, but your best bet is to head on over to this page and get a copy delivered right to your mailbox.
